About Me


Hey! I’m the guy you’re looking for. I’m a 22 year old videographer, editor and front-end web developer hailing from eastern Pennsylvania. I strive to create high-quality products and tell stories through the content and projects I manage. I leverage my background in film, graphic design, computer science, and interactive development to create and implement effective web strategies. My diverse background helps me tell the right story for your audience and take the project from beginning to end. Many people want a new website, but need the content for it too. I can assess your current site in terms of content, design, and data infrastructure and offer tangible improvements. Whether you want a new video testimonial, promotional photos or redesign of your site, I’m here to support you. One of my biggest passions is live streaming and creating content on the online platform, Twitch. I’ve been creating by brand there as “Sparkster” since 2013, and produce YouTube videos to support it. I have a go-getter personality and it drives me to support others through my creativity. I love what I do and I hope to support you and whatever you love to do.

I look forward to working with you in future endeavors!
